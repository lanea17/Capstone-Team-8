Project Description

Jakes on Devine is an innovative platform that aims to revolutionize the way restaurant orders are managed, focusing on simplicity, speed, and customer satisfaction. The project leverages cutting-edge technologies and modern UI/UX principles to deliver a seamless experience for both restaurant staff and customers.

Month 1: Planning & Setup

During Month 1, we focused on laying the foundation for the project, including the initial planning, technology stack selection, and setting up the project management systems.

Month 2: Development

Month 2 was all about development. We implemented the core features of the platform, including the order management system and the restaurant interface.

Month 3: Testing & Finalization

In the final month, we focused on testing, debugging, and polishing the system, ensuring that the platform was user-friendly and ready for deployment.
